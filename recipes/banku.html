What is Banku

So Today’s Recipe Is Banku, So I’ll share with you all my information about What Is Banku, How TO make it, the ingredients,and what can you serve with This Delicious Dish

Ingredients

  • Cassava dough
  • Corn dough
  • salt

Steps

Now am going to share with you how to make a banku Ghanaian recipe Step by step. if you trying this for the first time just focus and follow the steps, because my first time was a disaster so I don’t want you to make my fault
  1. first, in a pot mix the corn and cassava dough with water until smooth, start with 1 cup of water then if you notice that still hard just add slowly until smooth.
  2. over high heat stirring continuously.
    3. Depending on how moist the processed dough was at the start, you can add more water may so far as the consistency is solid, but not too firm.l Turn Down the heat to medium and allow the banku to steam in the pot, surrounded by 2 tablespoons of water. This should take 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Stir for about 1 minute then turn off the heat.
  4. add the desired amount into a wet bowl and swirl around until you make a ball.
  5. Serve The banku balls with your favorite Dish I recommend you to serve it with okro stew or Grilled fish.
